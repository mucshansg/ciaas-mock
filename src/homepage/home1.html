One program, three surfaces

A control plane for you, a wallet for them, oversight for everyone.

Every card program needs three different views into the same ledger. Paymentech ships all three, pre-wired to one source of truth.

01 · For your team

Operator dashboard

Issue cards, fund the program, and watch authorizations land in real time — from one workspace.

  • Real-time auth and decline feed
  • Program balance and same-day funding
  • Card issuance and lifecycle actions
  • Spend alerts and verification queue
02 · For their customer

Customer portal

A self-serve wallet your customers sign into to manage the card you issued them.

  • Passwordless sign-in via one-time code
  • Card details, masked PAN, live balance
  • Report lost or stolen, instantly
  • Transaction history with merchant detail
03 · For compliance

Ops console

Cross-program visibility for the team accountable to your card network and your auditors.

  • Cross-business search, any card or customer
  • Append-only audit trail on every action
  • Suspend, reactivate, force-decline
  • Exportable history for network audits

"Suspended" should mean something — and prove it happened.

Every card on Paymentech moves through a defined state machine, not a status field someone can quietly edit. Each transition is decided idempotently, the way a real issuer's authorization engine has to behave, and is written once to an append-only log that your ops team and your auditors can both trust.
